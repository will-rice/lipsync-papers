# Evaluation

We evaluate Gemino in a simulation environment and atop a WebRTC-based implementation. We describe our setup in §<a href="#sec:eval_setup" data-reference-type="ref" data-reference="sec:eval_setup">[sec:eval_setup]</a> and use it to compare existing baselines in §<a href="#sec:eval_overall" data-reference-type="ref" data-reference="sec:eval_overall">[sec:eval_overall]</a>. §<a href="#sec:eval_model" data-reference-type="ref" data-reference="sec:eval_model">[sec:eval_model]</a> motivates our model design, §<a href="#sec:eval_config" data-reference-type="ref" data-reference="sec:eval_config">[sec:eval_config]</a> discusses the impact of having the codec in our training process, and §<a href="#sec:eval_adaptation" data-reference-type="ref" data-reference="sec:eval_adaptation">[sec:eval_adaptation]</a> shows that Gemino closely matches a time-varying target bitrate.
